How to fill your timesheet
  • Date: The day you worked.
  • Client: Client name as agreed for this shift (free text).
  • Job Type: Pick from the list set by admin. This determines your dev-rate fallback.
  • Scheduled/Actual: Times you agreed and actually worked.
  • Break (mins): Unpaid break time in minutes.
  • Rate/Pay: Editable. Pay auto-calculates, but you can override.
  • Travel/Parking (£): Parking, tolls, etc.
  • Notes: Anything approvers should know.
  • Your entries auto-save after a short pause. Submit when done.
