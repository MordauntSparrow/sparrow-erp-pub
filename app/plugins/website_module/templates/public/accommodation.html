Accommodation at The George & Dragon

Stay in the heart of the Sussex countryside

Escape to the tranquillity of Dragons Green and enjoy a peaceful stay at The George & Dragon, our beautifully restored 16th-century country pub in Shipley, West Sussex. Surrounded by rolling countryside, walking routes, historic villages and cosy local charm, our accommodation offers the perfect blend of comfort, character and relaxation.

Whether you’re exploring West Sussex, attending a local event, visiting family, or simply treating yourself to a countryside break, our rooms provide a warm and welcoming place to rest.
