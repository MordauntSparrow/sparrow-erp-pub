About The George & Dragon

Welcome to The George & Dragon — a cosy, rustic country pub with 200 years of history nestled in the heart of Shipley, Horsham. We are a family-owned establishment, proudly serving real ales, locally sourced ingredients, and traditional British favourites with a modern twist.

Our menu is crafted by Chef Dominique Toussaint, a culinary expert with over 20 years’ experience in Michelin-starred restaurants and fine dining across the UK. Whether you’re popping in for a quiet pint, a hearty Sunday roast, or a special evening out, our warm wooden interiors and charming pub garden provide the perfect setting.

We are dog-friendly and offer garden seating when weather permits. Our seasonal menus spotlight the best of West Sussex produce, while our real ales and local ciders (including the full draught Hawkstone range) reflect our commitment to backing British farming.

Curious about our more spooky side? Explore ghost stories and legends that have been passed down for generations, revealing the pub’s colourful past and the spirits that may still roam our halls.
