UK compliance context (CQC, HSE / Purple Guide themes, indicative)
  • CQC: Vehicles used to transport patients to hospital are regulated; providers offering ambulances for transfers should be appropriately registered where the law applies.
  • HSE / Purple Guide themes: Ambulances are not the main treatment area. Plan a proper medical post so vehicles stay free for emergencies and transfers.
  • Tiering: Tiers 1–5 summarise typical expectations from published event-medical tier guidance (duration, crowd, alcohol/drugs, activity risk, transfers). Your SAG or licensing authority may expect a written MNA.

Numbers here are a planning aid only. They are not clinical advice and do not replace your risk assessment, insurer, or medical sign off.
