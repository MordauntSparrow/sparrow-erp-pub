Emails & realtime alerts

Applicants receive email when they apply, when the stage changes, when interview details are set, when pre-hire items are approved or rejected, when a form is assigned, and when hired. Outgoing email and public links in those messages depend on your server configuration. Internal copies go to users who have any Recruitment permission (not every administrator); superuser accounts are included by default. The recruitment panel can show live updates for the same internal audience.

Applicant data retention

After {{ applicant_retention_days }} days, hired or rejected applications are purged from the recruitment portal; copies already moved to HR or the employee record are kept.
