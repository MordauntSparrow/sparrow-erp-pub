Two tracks: consumables vs equipment

Inventory command centre

Consumables — bulk and small items (batches, expiry, quantities). Equipment — larger serial-tracked kit (assignments, servicing). Same locations and transactions engine underneath; different entry points so stores stay clear.

Inventory guide How consumables, equipment, and day-to-day workflows fit together — click or tap to expand.
  1. Bulk parts / consumables — work from Consumables; quantities live in Locations. Receipts and issues → Transactions; lot-level traceability and expiry → Batches.
  2. Medicines and dated consumables — same track as other consumable SKUs: Consumables + Batches for expiry and FEFO. Scene documentation stays in Cura / ePCR; inventory is the shelf truth.
  3. Serial kit & larger equipment — use Equipment (desk + Add equipment for product line + first unit). Import can still load consumable or serial rows from CSV. Assign to contractor, staff, vehicle, or holding / site; Sign back in{% if asset_links_available %} from Assignments or{% endif %} the equipment desk.
  4. Holding pool — kit signed out to a Holding or Virtual location stays visible below until someone verifies condition, logs faults, and puts it away. Sparrow can create a default holding location for you when you first use this workflow.
  5. Contractor kit requests — field staff submit dates and needs via the portal; stores fulfil and mark Kit requests fulfilled or declined (audit: who resolved, when).
  6. Crew / jobs (who is on which incident){% if ventus_links_available %} — Ventus · Jobs and Units for dispatch; pair that with equipment assigned to vehicle here for a full picture.{% else %} — when Ventus is enabled, open Jobs / Units for dispatch-side crew assignment.{% endif %}
  7. Parts fitted onto a vehicle (mechanical BOM){% if fleet_links_available %} — Fit part to vehicle or Fleet → Vehicles → Vehicle parts.{% else %} — enable Fleet for chassis parts separate from serial kit.{% endif %}
  8. Equipment warranty & service — Warranty until and service targets on each serial asset; this dashboard surfaces the next 90 days.
